Adding terminology for a more professional vocabulary:

1) Epoch - The exact time associated with a state
2) State	The spacecraft's position and velocity at an instant
3) Trajectory -	The path through space over time
4) Orbit - A trajectory that repeats around a central body
5) Maneuver -	An intentional change in velocity (ΔV)
6) Propagation - Predicting future motion from current conditions
7) Force Model - Everything acting on the spacecraft
8) Reference Frame - The coordinate system used to describe motion
9) State Vector - Position + velocity
10) Telemetry - Housekeeping and payload data from spacecraft
11) Engineering assumptions - information that is already known
